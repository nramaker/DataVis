Can Employee Departures Be Pedicted Using HR Data?


Employee retention is a focal issue for many large companies, especially those in the technical industry where employees have many opportunities available to them. Hiring a new employee is a long and expensive process, and once hired a company must invest several weeks or months of training into the employee before he or she will become a productive contributor. This cost and loss of productivity is compounded when the new employee is intended to replace a highly productive employee who has decided to leave the company due to low job satisfaction or to pursue a higher salary.

To minimize the impact from employee turnover, many Human Resource departments are examining their employee data to find patterns in employee departures. One such dataset was publicly released as part of a Kaggle competition to predict employee departures using machine learning, and can be downloaded from the following link: https://www.kaggle.com/ludobenistant/hr-analytics
